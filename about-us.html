About Us

Welcome to tnclassroom, a dedicated digital platform designed to empower students and teachers across Tamil Nadu.

Founded by the Teachers' Network for Skills & Education Development, Thanjavur—who are also behind the official YouTube channel TNSED - THANJAVUR—our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible, and free educational resources.

We specialize in materials for:

  • Tamil Nadu School Education (6th to 12th Std)
  • TNTET & TRB Exam Preparation
  • Psychology and Pedagogy Sessions

As educators, we understand the challenges of finding organized study materials. tnclassroom is built to bridge that gap, offering structured lessons, video tutorials, and practice tests to help every learner succeed.
